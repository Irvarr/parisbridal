About Us

Your One-Stop Destination for Weddings & Quinceañeras in Roswell, NM

Welcome to Paris Bridal, a family-owned bridal boutique located in the heart of Roswell, NM. For years, we have proudly served brides, quinceañeras, and families across Roswell and surrounding areas, helping them celebrate their most special moments with elegance and style.

Our Services

  • Bridal Gowns – Timeless, elegant, and modern designs for every bride.
  • Quinceañera Dresses – Stunning gowns in a variety of colors and styles.
  • Tuxedo Rentals – Stylish and affordable options for grooms, chambelanes, and formal events.
  • Evening Wear & Shoes – Perfect for weddings, parties, and galas.
  • Accessories – Veils, tiaras, jewelry, and more to complete your look.
  • Venue Decorations – Custom packages for weddings, quinceañeras, and special events.
  • Wedding Planning Services – Helping you create the perfect event, stress-free.
  • Gift Registry – Now offering a wedding registry to make gift-giving easier.

Why Choose Paris Bridal?

  • Locally Owned & Family-Operated – We bring a personal touch to every celebration.
  • Exclusive Selection – Handpicked high-quality dresses, accessories, and décor.
  • Affordable & Customizable – We work within your budget to bring your vision to life.
  • Personalized Service – Our experienced team offers one-on-one consultations.

Visit us at 106 South Main St, Roswell, NM—just across from the UFO Museum.
